How does it works?

Create playlists by importing music from YouTube, your hard drive or any distant URL and let your friends vote from their smartphone for the music they want to listen to. Anyone can also propose a new song via an integrated YouTube search.

Why should I use it?

It's fast, simple, and especially useful. Your friends can access the vote page just by scanning a QR code. The event creator
